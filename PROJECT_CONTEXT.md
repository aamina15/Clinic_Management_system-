# ClinicFlow AI

ClinicFlow AI is a production-ready SaaS healthcare platform designed for clinics, hospitals, and diagnostic centers.

## Goal

Build a secure, scalable, enterprise-grade healthcare management platform.

## Users

- Admin
- Doctor
- Receptionist
- Patient
- Pharmacist (future)
